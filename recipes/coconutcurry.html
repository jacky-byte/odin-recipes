Lemongrass Coconut Curry

Home

Description

This versatile coconut curry recipe is incredibly easy to make vegetarian (using vegetable stock, omitting the chicken, not using fish sauce, etc.).

Ingredients

  • Coconut Milk
  • Protein of Choice
  • Lemongrass
  • Garlic
  • Chili Flakes
  • Onions
  1. Fry protein on high heat, for sealing and maillard effect
  2. Remove protein
  3. Fry aromatics on low heat to activate the flavours
  4. Add all ingredients together, slow cook duration depending on cut of protein
